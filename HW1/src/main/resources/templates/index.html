In this website you can search for covid information, by country and by date.

Statistics

You can check all countries statistics, which will display all numbers about every country.

Or you can also add a country name, to get the data on country.
Country History

You can check a country's history by giving the country, which will display all data about that country.

Or you can also add a date, to get the data on a specific date.
